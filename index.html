How to fly

Drag to look · W A S D fly · Q E up/down · Shift boost · scroll speed · Space / click hold fire lasers · V cockpit view · N warp to mission target · click anything to inspect, then Warp. Watch for asteroids in the belts — shoot them (+25) or eat hull damage.

Lensing quality: AUTO tunes ray-march steps to your GPU every frame (great for integrated graphics); or force HIGH / LOW / OFF.

Land & walk: on rocky worlds press 🚶 in the info card. On foot: WASD walk, Space jump (real gravity per world!), Esc to lift off.

Scale: every size, distance and position is real (NASA/JPL, Gaia). Directions and apparent sizes are always true; beyond ~67 AU radial distance is log-compressed so the observable universe fits on your screen — the HUD always shows true distances. Objects marked HYPOTHETICAL are ideas, not observations.

Black holes render with ray-traced gravitational lensing — fly toward one.

Planet & Moon imagery: NASA mission mosaics via Solar System Scope (CC-BY 4.0). Data: NASA/JPL, ESA Gaia, EHT.
